📘 How SafeSakhi Works

SafeSakhi is a personal safety app designed for women to send emergency alerts quickly.

🚨 When You Press the SOS Button:

  • 📢 A loud alarm sound plays to alert nearby people.
  • 📱 WhatsApp opens with a ready-to-send message to your trusted contact.
  • 🔦 (Coming soon) Flashlight will blink to get attention in dark areas.
  • 📍 (Coming soon) Location sharing will help people find you faster.

🌙 Useful Even at Night (Like 3 AM)

Even if family is sleeping, the alarm helps neighbors notice something is wrong. WhatsApp message lets trusted people help you after they see it.

💡 Tip:

Add this app to your phone home screen for quick access in emergencies.
